About Dogtown Records

When we created this record store there was one thing we had in mind, and that was to stay true to our Californian roots. All workers and owners hail from California whether it be Northern, Southern and even Central. One important aspect was that we each had a love for either skateboarding or surfboarding, some could be both, one, or none but still held a love for both sports. Chosing what to call our store and finding an aestheic was the least difficult part. Lords Of Dogtown is a movie based on real skaters who were also surfers and thous gave us our store DogTown. The vision for our store was simple. Create a place for those who loved the lifestyle of surf and skate as much as we do. Deciding to become a record store was an idea we all thought about but could never quite see us going through with. In the end we t ook a chance and that chance eventually paid off. Our record store is inspired by the wierdos, misfits, and all inbetween. Dogtown is a one of a kind type of record store only to be seenhere in sunny California. Our misson for this record store is to give back to the people who helped make our dreams become a reality. We bring the artist you love and make it possible for our customers to see those artist with live events, and live interviews for them to be apart of. Our record store also brings the music to you. Dogtown has a Classic blue camper travel to beaches and areas like the Santa Monica peir and Venice Beach to allow customers to listen to and buy records as well as buy clothing. The camper has two sets of racks out with our clothing and records will be kept inside the camper as well as on the doors. A seating area will be provided for customers to enjoy the music and hang out. We want for everyone to experince who we are and what we have to offer.
